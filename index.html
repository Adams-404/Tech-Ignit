Our Vision:

Our vision at GDG GSU is to ignite a passion and leadership in community!

Tech Ignit is an annual conference for tech enthusiasts from novices to industry leaders organized by the Google Developer Group Gombe (GDG Gombe). The event features sessions on various topics, including AI/ML, Mobile Development, Design, Web Development, Cloud Computing, DevOps, Cybersecurity, and more.
