About GDG GSU

As the Google Developer Group (GDG) at Gombe State University, we are dedicated to empowering students and fostering a vibrant tech community through hands-on learning and access to cutting-edge technology.

Event Purpose

Our purpose is to ignite passion & innovation within GSU students, celebrate the culmination of our GDG Tech Academy's first pathway, honor their achievements, and provide them with hands-on experience through Google's cutting-edge AI initiatives.
