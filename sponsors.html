About GDG GSU

As the Google Developer Group (GDG) at Gombe State University, we are dedicated to empowering students and fostering a vibrant tech community through hands-on learning and access to cutting-edge technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about sponsoring Tech Ignite 2025

What is the deadline for sponsorship applications?

We accept sponsorship applications on a rolling basis, but we recommend applying at least 4 weeks before the event to ensure maximum branding opportunities and proper integration into our marketing materials.

Can we customize our sponsorship package?

We understand that every organization has unique goals and requirements. We're happy to work with you to create a custom sponsorship package that aligns with your objectives and budget.

What promotional materials do you provide to sponsors?

Sponsors receive a comprehensive promotional package including high-resolution event photos, social media content, attendee demographics report, and post-event impact analysis. Higher-tier sponsors also receive video highlights and press coverage summaries.

How do you measure sponsorship ROI?

We provide detailed analytics including social media reach, engagement metrics, lead generation numbers, brand mention tracking, and attendee feedback surveys. We also offer post-event consultation to help you assess the impact of your sponsorship.

Can international companies sponsor the event?

Yes! We welcome international sponsors and can accommodate remote participation through virtual presentations, online exhibitions, and digital branding opportunities. We'll work with you to ensure maximum impact regardless of your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, online payments, and can accommodate international wire transfers. For in-kind sponsorships, we'll work with you to establish the equivalent value and documentation requirements.
